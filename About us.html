Flexible online Bus Booking eases booking of buses at the convenience of someone's own. We are here to reduce congestion at bus terminals.

VISION

To be the leading bus company.

MISSION

To provide the fastest and easiest way to get a ticket.

OBJECTIVES

- To design and develop a bus booking system in Uganda.
- To allow travelers to book tickets at any time and any place.
- To streamline the work of bus companies and travelers.

HISTORY

Flexible Bus Company was founded in 2023 by Nasaka Suzanne, a young and ambitious entrepreneur with a passion for transportation. Nasaka saw an opportunity to provide a more flexible and customer-centric bus service in Uganda and across East Africa. Nasaka Suzanne had over 10 years of experience in the transportation industry, having worked for several major bus companies in Uganda. She was known for her innovative ideas and her commitment to providing excellent customer service. In 2023, Nasaka decided to start her own bus company, Flexible Bus Company. She wanted to create a company that would offer customers more flexibility and choice, as well as a better overall travel experience. Flexible Bus Company quickly became one of the most popular bus companies in Uganda. Nasaka's commitment to providing excellent customer service and her innovative ideas paid off. Flexible Bus Company was known for its: Flexible ticketing options: Flexible Bus Company offered customers a variety of ticketing options, including online booking, mobile booking, and over-the-counter booking. Customers could also purchase tickets on board the bus. Comfortable buses: Flexible Bus Company's buses were all equipped with comfortable seats, air conditioning, and entertainment systems. Reliable service: Flexible Bus Company had a reputation for being a reliable and on-time bus company. Affordable fares: Flexible Bus Company offered competitive fares on all of its routes. In addition to its domestic routes, Flexible Bus Company also offered international routes to several countries in East Africa, including Kenya, Burundi, South Sudan, Tanzania, and Rwanda. In 2026, Flexible Bus Company was named the "Best Bus Company in Uganda" by the Uganda National Chamber of Commerce and Industry. This award was a testament to Nasaka's hard work and dedication to providing excellent customer service. Under Nasaka's leadership, Flexible Bus Company has grown into one of the leading bus companies in Uganda and East Africa. The company is committed to providing customers with a flexible, comfortable, and reliable travel experience.
